Ingredients

2 cups of flour

2 tbsp sugar

1/4 tsp of nutmeg

1/2 tsp salt

1/4 cuo of melted butter

1 cup of milk

Method

boil your eggs in salted water for about 10mins

serve and combine all dry ingredients in a bowl

add in meltd butter and mix with your hands
